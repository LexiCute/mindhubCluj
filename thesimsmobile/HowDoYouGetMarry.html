Once you reach level 7 in the game and your sims reach level 4 in their romantic relationship you will get the option to complete the social event 'Propose' once you complete this you will be able to get your sims married!



The Quest has to be completed in the allotted time but you can still have your Sims marry by placing all 3 items in the yard and then by tapping on the arch. Make sure the Sim that initiated the relationship is the one selected when you tap on the arch.
